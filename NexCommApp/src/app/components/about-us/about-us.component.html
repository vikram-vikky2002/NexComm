About NexComm

NexComm is built with a mission to ensure seamless communication even in areas with limited or no internet connectivity.

🚀 Our Mission

NexComm was created to solve a real-world problem — poor internet connectivity in remote areas. We aim to provide an offline-first messaging solution that enables users to chat and share files over a local area network (LAN), ensuring fast, secure, and reliable communication without the internet.
