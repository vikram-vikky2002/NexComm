Help Center

Need assistance? Find answers to the most common questions about NexComm here.

💬 What is NexComm?

NexComm is an offline communication app that allows users to chat and share files over a local area network (LAN) without internet access.

📡 How does LAN communication work?

NexComm detects other users connected to the same local network and enables direct messaging and file sharing through peer-to-peer connections.

👥 How can I create a group chat?

From the main interface, go to "Create Group", choose participants from the available users, and start a conversation instantly.

📁 Can I send files?

Yes! Simply open a chat (personal or group) and click on the "Send File" button to transfer files directly over LAN.

🔒 Is my data secure?

NexComm ensures that all communications stay within your LAN. No external servers or cloud storage — your data never leaves your network.
